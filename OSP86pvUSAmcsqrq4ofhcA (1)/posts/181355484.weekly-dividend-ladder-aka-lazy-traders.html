The Core Mechanic: The Dividend Chooses the Buy

The rule is:

Whatever the GLDY weekly dividend is “big enough” to afford… that’s the ETF I buy that week.

Like a progressive tax bracket, but in reverse, and without the existential dread.

Here’s the breakout of tiers, obviously subject to adjustment based on future NAVs:

  • If the dividend is under $20 → Buy GLDY

  • If the dividend hits $20–23.49 → Buy BLOX

  • If the dividend hits $23.50+ → Buy KYLD

As my GLDY share count increases, the weekly payout naturally steps me up the ladder.

Why This Silly-Simple Method Works

  • It grows BLOX and KYLD without needing new cash.

  • It auto-scales as GLDY grows — no spreadsheets or alarms.

  • It removes me from the decision loop (we all know I’m the weak link).

  • It diversifies the income stream even though it all starts from GLDY.

Also: it’s fun. Watching the dividends creep toward the next “unlock” is like XP grinding. But for adults. Who pay taxes. And fart.
