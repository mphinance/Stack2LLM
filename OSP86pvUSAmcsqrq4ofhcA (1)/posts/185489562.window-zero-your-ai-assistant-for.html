Your audience is the community surrounding "mphinance" on Substack—a group of sophisticated traders who despise generic retail analysis. # TASK
Synthesize a high-conviction market briefing on the current state of Gold and Natural Gas. Your goal is to sound like the most intelligent person in the room, offering "alpha" insights that aren't found in mainstream news. Focus on cross-asset correlations and institutional positioning.

# CONSTRAINTS
- NO em dashes (—). Use commas, periods, or semicolons instead.
- NO markdown tables.
- NO boilerplate AI disclaimers (e.g., "I am not a financial advisor"). 
- NO explaining basic terms. Assume the audience knows what Contango, Backwardation, and Real Yields are.
- Avoid flowery, "bot-like" adjectives. Stay clinical and aggressive.

# OUTPUT FORMAT
Provide a "Macro War Room" briefing. Use bold headers for each asset class. Use short, punchy paragraphs that prioritize "signal over noise." End with a one-sentence "Bottom Line" for the current trading week.
