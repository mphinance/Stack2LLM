Total Capital Outlay

How to Read the Graph: The “Over/Under” Line

The orange line is our “Over/Under” profit goal. To stay in the green, HOOD’s price simply needs to stay above that line on any given date.

  • The Blue Line (Stock): This is the boring way. It’s a flat line at $118.13. If the stock is above that, you’re in profit.

  • The Orange Line (Options): This line “climbs” over time. This represents the “rent” (time decay) we pay for having 2x the share exposure.

  • The Goal: On Jan 15, 2027, the “Final Over” mark is $148.90.
